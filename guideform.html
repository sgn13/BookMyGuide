About the company

This website aims to contribute to our tourism industry especially the ‘Visit Nepal 2020’ campaign by allowing the tourists to choose guides as per their needs.The profiles of the guides as well as the ratings and reviews to help them make their decision.The guides will also have a greater exposure and will be able to freelance or work full time in the site.Our registration system and payment system is set up in such a way that it will prevent both customer and guide from fraud.Overall this website is meant to give the customers more freedom over the guides they choose.
